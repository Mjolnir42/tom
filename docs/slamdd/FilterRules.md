# Filter Rules

```
DROP; ProtocolID; $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
DROP; Protocol;   $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
DROP; TcpFlags;   $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
DROP; SrcAddress; $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
DROP; SrcPort;    $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
DROP; DstAddress; $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
DROP; DstPort;    $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
DROP; IPVersion;  $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...

PASS; ProtocolID; $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
PASS; Protocol;   $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
PASS; TcpFlags;   $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
PASS; SrcAddress; $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
PASS; SrcPort;    $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
PASS; DstAddress; $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
PASS; DstPort;    $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...
PASS; IPVersion;  $value,$value,... | NOT $value,$value,...

SET; AgentID;    $value | clientIP
SET; SrcAddress; $value | clientIP
SET; DstAddress; $value | clientIP

REPLACE; SrcAddress; $old,$old,... | NOT $old,$old,...; $new
REPLACE; SrcPort;    $old,$old,... | NOT $old,$old,...; $new
REPLACE; DstAddress; $old,$old,... | NOT $old,$old,...; $new
REPLACE; DstPort;    $old,$old,... | NOT $old,$old,...; $new
```
